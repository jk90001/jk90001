Our Wedding

We are delighted to invite you to the wedding of John and Louise on the 15th of April 2021 in Beverley, Western Australia.

The ceremony will commence at 3pm at the Beverley Station Art Gallery. Please arrive at 2:30pm to be seated for the beginning of the ceremony. A reception will follow at the Beverley Town Hall at 6pm. The two venues are comfortably within walking distance of each other, approximately 200 metres apart.

We will also be offering canapés in between the ceremony and reception at the Freemasons Tavern, Beverley

Please note that although we love your children, we regretfully cannot accommodate them at these venues. Babies in arms are however welcome.

The dress code is Cocktail/Semi-Formal.

We would love for you to join us in celebrating this special day and hope to see you there. We ask that you please RSVP by the 1st of March 2021.

Gifts

We understand that many of you will be traveling far and wide to join us on our special day, and that is the greatest gift we could ask for.

Therefore, our only wish is to see you on the day and no gifts are needed or expected.

If you still feel as though you would like to give us something, contributions towards our honeymoon fund would be very much appreciated. However, please don’t feel expected to contribute.

The Location

Beverley is a peaceful one and a half hour drive from Perth through the Helena National Park or Mundaring State Forrest.

Accommodation

Near the town of Beverley is a larger country town called York, a 20 minute drive from Beverley. Both towns have a variety of accommodation options to suit any budget. However, if you are looking for a little more choice, or maybe a little luxury, we recommend staying in York. To help you out we have listed links to some accommodation options below.
