The Location

Beverley is a peaceful one and a half hour drive from Perth through the Helena National Park or Mundaring State Forrest. Once you get here take a stroll down the main street of the idyllic country town and admire the variety of architecture, from Art Deco to Georgian to the newly built Cornerstone building, home of the visitor centre.
