Wildlife

For our friends & family visiting the West Coast for the first time we want you to experience what the West side has to offer and give you some local tips on what to check out. I'm sure you have all heard us bang on about how good WA is, so we don’t want you to be disappointed. We’ve listed some links and tips on things to check out while you are in the land of the red dirt.

Rottnest Island

A standard for any tourist coming over to the west coast. Get a selfie with a quokka and snorkel in the incredible clear blue water reefs. Here’s some links to some day trips you can do over to Rotto:

  • www.getyourguide.co.uk/fremantle-l1380/from-fremantle-rottnest-ferry-bike-hire-package-t351171/
  • www.getyourguide.co.uk/fremantle-l1380/from-fremantle-rottnest-island-ferry-admission-t343111/
  • rottnestexpress.com.au/experiences/day-packages/experience-rottnest/
  • rottnestexpress.com.au/experiences/day-packages/experience-rottnest-with-snorkel-hire/
Penguin Island

This is one of the most underrated little adventures that you can do just 45 minutes out from Perth CBD. Penguin island is just a five-minute ferry ride away from Shoalwater. The wildlife is incredible, with wild dolphins, rare Australian sea lions and the world’s smallest penguins! There’s heaps of different packages and things you can book so check out the different options here:

www.penguinisland.com.au/
Perth Zoo

One of the best zoos in Australia so definitely recommend checking out if you are staying for a little while.

perthzoo.wa.gov.au/

www.cavershamwildlife.com.au/

Must See Sights and Activities in Perth
The Sunset!

Great at any of our recommended beaches but City Beach would be our top pick, you can take a picnic and sit on the grass or grab some fish & chips at Clancy’s Fish Pub

King's Park

One of the largest inner city parks in the world with insane views of the city and the Swan River, war memorial, native gardens, various bush walks a café and a restaurant.

www.bgpa.wa.gov.au/kings-park
The Swan Valley

WA's oldest wine region just 25 minutes from the Perth CBD. Lots of great wineries, breweries and restaurants here.

www.swanvalley.com.au/Home
Margaret River

Amazing beaches, great old growth forest, caves and of course fantastic wineries in a picturesque region 3 hours south of Perth.

www.margaretriver.com/towns/margaret-river/
Bold Park

A great bush walk and lookout. Recommend visiting on the way to City Beach for post-walk swim!

www.bgpa.wa.gov.au/bold-park
Fremantle

really cool old portside town with lots of cafes, bars, nightlife, markets and breweries

www.experienceperth.com/region/fremantle
Hillary's Boat Harbour
www.hillarysboatharbour.com.au/
